Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Senior Data Analyst with a data engineering focus, bridging the gap between raw data and strategic business outcomes. With 15+ years of experience across telecommunications (AT&T), retail (Signet Jewelers), healthcare, and finance, I specialize in building scalable data pipelines, automating workflows, and creating actionable insights that drive measurable results.

My expertise spans the full data lifecycle—from architecting AWS/Redshift pipelines and Snowflake data warehouses to developing predictive ML models and executive dashboards in Power BI and Tableau. At Signet Jewelers, I led the deduplication of 150M+ records, building production-ready ETL pipelines with AWS Glue and Salesforce integrations. At AT&T, I automated data ingestion workflows, reducing manual tasks by 40% while improving dashboard performance by 25%.

I thrive in collaborative environments, partnering with cross-functional stakeholders to translate complex business requirements into data solutions. Whether it's optimizing financial models, analyzing marketing campaign ROI, or forecasting project timelines with machine learning, I deliver insights that empower data-driven decision-making.
